Peanut Butter Kiss Cookies

Description

Simple and delicious dessert! Peanut butter cookies made with just five ingredients. Once they are finished baking a chocolate kiss is pressed into the middle to give it a little extra touch!

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Sugar
  • One egg, room temp
  • Vanilla extract
  • Milk chocolate kisses
Steps
  1. Mix the ingredients together, excluding the chocolate kisses.
  2. Once mixed, roll into 1-1/4in balls.
  3. Place the balls on baking pan about 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until tops of cookies are slightly cracked.
  5. While still hot, press 1 chocolate kiss into the center of each cookie.
  6. Let cool, and Enjoy!
