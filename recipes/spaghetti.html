Spaghetti

Description

This Spaghetti recipe is loved by many as it uses basic household ingredients! Topping cooked spaghetti noodles with a rich and heavy homemade meatsauce takes less than 45 minutes! It can be paired with either a salad or garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Veggies
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Beef broth
  • Tomatoes
  • Italian seasoning
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Bay leaves
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese & Fresh basil
Steps
  1. Heat olive oil over med-high heat. Add in veggies and cook until soft. Stir in garlic and cook for about one minute.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned. While it is cooking break beef into smaller pieces. Drain grease one done.
  3. Pour in beef broth and bring to a boil. Let simmer for two minutes.
  4. Add in seasoning and tomatoes. Stir well to combine and add bay leaves.
  5. Reduce heat and let simmer for about 20 minutes.
  6. While sauce is on simmer, cook spaghetti noodles according to instructions on package.
  7. Remove bay leaves.
  8. Plate spaghetti noodles and pour the meatsauce over the noodles.
  9. Garnish with parmesan cheese and basil.
